Brinley Francis Portfolio

A showcase of creativity, skill, and digital growth

Site Name

Brinley Francis Portfolio — a personal portfolio website showcasing selected projects in writing, social media management, web development, and customer support. The name reflects my identity and the personal nature of the work presented.

Optional domain: brinleyfrancis.com (available)

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to serve as a digital portfolio that highlights my skills and experience across different creative and technical fields. It allows potential clients, employers, and collaborators to explore my work, learn about my background, and contact me for opportunities. The site will feature examples of writing, social media content, and projects developed during my software development coursework.

Scenarios

  • “I’m looking for someone who can manage social media accounts and create engaging content — what experience does Brinley have in this area?”
  • “Can I see examples of Brinley’s web development projects and writing samples?”
  • “How do I contact Brinley for potential freelance or remote opportunities?”

Color Schema

The color palette was chosen to maintain a modern and professional look with soft, warm undertones.

  • Primary: #4A4E69 – used for headings and navigation.
  • Accent: #9A8C98 – used for buttons, highlights, and borders.
  • Background: #F2E9E4 – used for page background.

Typography

The chosen font is Roboto, a clean sans-serif typeface that is professional and easy to read.

  • Headings: Roboto Bold
  • Body Text: Roboto Regular
  • Accent Text: Roboto Medium (for emphasis and buttons)
